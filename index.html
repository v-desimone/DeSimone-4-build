About Us

VD's Bakery was founded in May of 2026 by VD, an avid home baker. She wanted to share her love of baking with the world, so she decided to launch VD's Bakery in January of 2026. Here at VD's Bakery, we specialize in making cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. All of our products are 100% plant based, and completely dairy free! Our menu selection changes regularly, so make sure to check back here often to see our newest menu items!
